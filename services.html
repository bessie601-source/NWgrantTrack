How It Works

A straightforward process designed for busy community volunteers and small organisations.

1

Initial Consultation (Free)

We discuss your project idea, timeline, and funding needs. I will recommend suitable grants and the best service level for your situation.

2

Eligibility Check

Before we start writing, we confirm you (or your partner organisation) meet all eligibility criteria. No point writing for a grant you cannot receive.

3

Document Gathering

You provide project details, budgets, quotes, and any existing materials. I handle the formatting, narrative development, and alignment with assessment criteria.

4

Draft & Revise

You receive a complete draft for review. We iterate based on your feedback until you are confident in the submission.

5

Submission

Final documents prepared, application submitted, and confirmation provided. Full Service clients also receive post-submission follow-up support.
