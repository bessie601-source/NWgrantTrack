# North West Grant Services - Business Strategy Summary

## Executive Summary

**Business Model**: Grant writing services for environmental and community organizations in NSW North West, using a professional Grant Tracker tool as a competitive differentiator.

**Decision**: Sell services (outcome), not tool subscriptions.

---

## Your Competitive Advantages

1. **2023 Sydney Region Landcare Community Group Award** — Instant credibility
2. **10+ years managing community native plant nursery** — Proven delivery capability
3. **Deep local knowledge** — North West NSW communities, networks, needs
4. **Aboriginal partnerships** — Connection to Traditional Owners for cultural knowledge projects
5. **Farmer neighbor** — Access to agricultural community for drought resilience work

---

## Business Structure Options

### Option 1: Sole Trader (Recommended for immediate start)
**Pros:**
- Start immediately with ABN
- No committee required
- Simple tax

**Cons:**
- Limited to grants accepting individuals (council grants mostly)
- Can't access major environmental funding

**Action:** Start here, incorporate later when you have committee

### Option 2: Incorporated Association
**Requirements:**
- Minimum 3 committee members (President, Secretary, Treasurer)
- Cost: $200-400 (NSW Fair Trading)
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks once committee formed

**Pros:**
- Access to ALL grants
- Can apply for DGR status (tax-deductible donations)
- Credibility with funders

**Cons:**
- Need 2 other people committed
- Governance overhead

**Action:** Build relationships that lead to committee

## Grant Tracker — Tool Strategy

**Purpose**: Competitive advantage, not product
- Demonstrates professionalism to clients
- Streamlines your workflow
- Builds authority in grant writing space

**Key Features Implemented:**
1. Eligibility tracking (color-coded badges)
2. Detailed grant criteria view
3. Document folder per grant
4. Calendar deadline view
5. Pipeline management (draft → submitted → outcome)
6. Grant templates (Landcare, Drought, Cultural, Native Plant)
7. Unsolicited proposals framework

**Data Persistence**: Local browser storage — survives browser restart
