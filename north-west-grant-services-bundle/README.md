# North West Grant Services - Complete Bundle

This package contains everything developed for your grant writing business and grant tracker tool for the NSW North West region.

## What's Included

### Services Website (`/services/`)
**File**: `services/index.html`

A professional landing page for your grant writing services featuring:
- One Tree Locale brand identity (golden yellow, olive green, forest green)
- Service tiers: Grant Review ($300), Grant Writing ($800-$1,500), Full Service ($2,000+)
- Credibility section highlighting your 2023 Sydney Region Landcare Award
- 5-step process from consultation to submission

**To customize**: Replace "grants@northwestservices.local" with your actual email and "0400 XXX XXX" with your phone number.

### Grant Tracker (`/grant-tracker/`)
**File**: `grant-tracker/index.html`

A complete web-based tool for managing grant applications with the following features:

#### Pre-loaded Grants (6 grants)
1. **Community Environment Program** - Australian Government ($2K-$20K)
2. **Indigenous Protected Areas Program** - NIAA ($50K+)
3. **Future Drought Fund** - Dept Agriculture ($10K-$100K)
4. **Local Land Services - Community Action** - NW LLS ($1K-$10K)
5. **Moree Plains Community Grants** - Council ($500-$5K) - *Individual eligible*
6. **Inverell Shire Community Development** - Council ($500-$3K) - *Individual eligible*

#### Features
- **Eligibility Tracking**: Color-coded badges showing who can apply
- **Detailed Grant View**: Full eligibility criteria, assessment criteria, description
- **Document Tracking**: Per-grant document folder to track application files
- **Calendar View**: Visual deadline tracking
- **Pipeline Management**: Track grants from draft → submitted → funded/rejected
- **Grant Templates**: Pre-built structures for Landcare, Drought Resilience, Cultural Knowledge, Native Plant applications
- **Unsolicited Proposals Tab**: Framework for direct pitches to NSW Government agencies

#### How to Use
1. Open `grant-tracker/index.html` in any web browser
2. Data saves automatically to browser local storage
3. Click "View Details" on any grant to see full information
4. Click "Track" to add to your pipeline
5. Switch to "My Pipeline" tab to manage applications in progress
6. Use "Unsolicited Proposals" tab for the direct pitch framework

## Key Business Insights from Research

### Your Situation
- **Immediate**: Sole trader status allows you to start grant writing services immediately
- **Grant Eligibility**: Most environmental grants require NFP/incorporated status (3+ person committee)
- **Individual Eligible Grants**: Council community grants (Moree Plains, Inverell) accept individuals

### Pathways to Funding
1. **Sole Trader Services** ($300-$2,000/project) - Immediate income
2. **Council Grants** ($500-$5,000) - Small projects, low competition
3. **Unsolicited Proposals** ($100K-$500K+) - 6-12 months, major initiatives
4. **Incorporation** - Opens all grants but requires 3-person committee

### Your Competitive Advantage
- 2023 Sydney Region Landcare Community Group Award winner
- 10+ years managing community native plant nursery
- Deep local knowledge of North West NSW communities
- Established relationships with Local Land Services
- Connections with Aboriginal organizations for partnership grants
